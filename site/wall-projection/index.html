The Wall
Projection

Artist Saloon & Film Screening

Da Capo Bar · Zürich HB · Sundays

A screening that is also a lecture, a lecture that is also a scroll. An artist presents their own work, or films that have shaped them, accompanied by short commentary — like a 2½-hour drift through UbuWeb with an audience, in a bar designed by Trix & Robert Haussmann that almost nobody finds.

Opening Session

The first presenter picks a thread — their work, their influences, their obsessions — and projects it onto the wall. Commentary between clips. No fixed format. The audience has drinks.

No. 002 TBD
Sunday, 13:00

Session Two

A different artist, a different wall. Could be 16mm transfers, could be screen recordings, could be a single durational piece with annotations whispered over the top.

No. 003 TBD
Sunday, 13:00

Session Three

The format settles or it doesn't. Each session is its own thing. The only constant: the wall, the bar, and the fact that almost nobody knows this place exists.
